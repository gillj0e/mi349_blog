Cookie Recipie

Joe Gill
Jun 8, 2025

Okay so the only annoying thing with this recipe is that if you really want it to be good you have to make the dough and then put it in the fridge for like two days. It helps the salt and butter and sugar mix and develop and it also firms up the cookie by cooling down the butter. You could make this recipe by putting the dough in the fridge for 2-6 hrs but I would recommend the full 48.

Makes 12-16 cookies

Ingredients

Portion/Measurment Ingredients
1 cup room temp/lightly microwaved salted butter
2 cup light brown sugar
OR 1 cup cup white sugar & 1 cup dark
1 tbs vanilla extract
2 cup light brown sugar
2 large room temp eggs (fill a cup with warm milk and put the eggs in it)
3 cups bread flower (NOT ALL PURPOSE UNLESS YOUâ€™RE A *****)
1 tsp baking soda
1/2 tsp baking powder
1 tbs kosher salt
2 cups dark chocolate chunks (milk choc is too sweet in this recipe)

Instructions

1. Do NOT preheat the oven, dough needs to chill.
2. Thoroughly cream together butter and sugar. This should take 5 min if done by hand.
3. Add in eggs and vanilla. Beat till combined.
4. Mix in flour, baking soda, salt and baking soda.
5. Mix in chocolate
6. Transfer dough to fridge safe container to chill.
7. Line a baking sheet with parchment and preheat oven to 365f. 
8. Make dough rounds that are roughly 1.5 in round.
9. Rounds will expand to be 2.5-3 in after baking, keep this in mind when placing rounds on the sheet.
10. Once yours sheets are ready place them in the fridge or freezer until they are ready to go in the oven. This helps the dough cook more thoroughly.
11. Place in oven for 8-12 min or until dough begins to brown.
12. Let them sit on the baking pan for 2 minutes before removing to cooling rack.

Tomato cream and basil vodka pasta recipe

Joe Gill
Jun 7, 2025 

Makes about 8 servings 

INGREDIENTS: 

6 tbs unsalted butter 
1 large yellow onion, diced
6 cloves garlic, diced
2 teaspoons red pepper flakes
12 oz can tomato paste 
2 cups heavy cream
2 lbs rigatoni pasta 
1/2 cup plain vodka
Basil 
Salt & pepper
Burrata and Parmesan cheese

DIRECTIONS:

Boil water for pasta in a large pan 

Dice 1 large yellow onion and 6 cloves garlic

Melt butter in medium/large saucepan 

Add onions and garlic to pan on medium heat 

Add red pepper and salt (Desired amount) and stir frequently until onions are soft but not brown, about 15 minutes 

Add tomato paste and stir until thick, about 10 minutes 

Add half of heavy whipping cream and stir before adding the other half 

Add vodka

Add noodles to boiling water in separate pan 

For sauce to be smooth-add to blender and blend until desired consistency

Try sauce and add more red pepper/salt if needed

Strain pasta water when rigatoni is Al Dante 

Add sauce to noodles

Garnish with basil and add Parmesan and burrata cheese on top
